The process

A simple three-step plan for small businesses

Most small businesses don't need complicated systems — they just need a clear path from attention to enquiry. This is the simple setup I recommend.

01
Attract

Short-form reels, promotional visuals, and regular content help your business stay visible. This builds awareness and trust before someone even clicks through to your site.

02
Convert

I build landing pages designed primarily for mobile, so everything is easy to read and quick to act on. Clear buttons — WhatsApp, call, or booking — are right at the top. No confusion, no digging around.

03
Manage

For businesses that take appointments, I recommend WorkSlot App. Customers choose a time quickly, you approve requests, and everyone stays organised. It also gives you a QR code and simple link — so customers can scan, save, and return to your booking page anytime.

Why it works

Why this works

Most websites fail because they try to do too much. They're cluttered, slow on mobile, and leave visitors unsure what to do next.

A better approach is simple: get attention on social media, send people to one clear mobile-friendly page, and give them an easy next step — WhatsApp, a phone call, or a booking link.

That's usually all you need to generate more enquiries consistently.

  • ✓ Designed mobile-first, for how your customers actually browse
  • ✓ Clear contact buttons right at the top — no scrolling required
  • ✓ Focused on one goal: getting visitors to contact or book
  • ✓ QR code and shareable link so customers can return easily
  • ✓ Simple enough for you to share and manage without any tech knowledge
